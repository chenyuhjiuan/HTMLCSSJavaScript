SheepFacts: A place to learn the facts of sheep

Welcome to SheepFacts. The purpose of SheepFacts is to teach students, teachers, beginning shepherds, and the general public about sheep and their products. The site uses simple language and pictures to illustrate the various topics. To begin learning about sheep, click on a link in the menu bar.

About the author. The author of SheepFacts is Emily Marinovich, Sheep & Goat Specialist at Jiu Chang Hong Enterprises. Emily has been a Sales Manager of international business in sheep products since 2010. She has B.S. degree in Nursing and M.S. degree in Human Genetics from Koahsiung Medical University and Howard University, respectively.
